## Fallen
### Spike Pit
- 1d6
- Can be jumped over or walked around
### Toxic Cave Gases
- 1d4 every round, halfed if mouth and nose are covered, immune with proper equipment
- Reducing Movement and Strength by 5 reduces damage by 1
